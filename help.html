Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a reminder?

Click the "Reminder" button in the sidebar, then fill out the form to create a new reminder.

How can I check the weather forecast?

Use the Weather button in the sidebar to open the dedicated weather page, which shows current conditions and a 5-day forecast.

How do I update my farm data?

Go to the "Manage Data" section from the sidebar, which opens a form for adding, editing, and deleting records.
